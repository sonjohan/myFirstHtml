How to make a sandwich to watch Dragon Ball

  1. Check for ingredients, 2 slices of bread, butter and ham
  2. If everything is available
    1. Spread butter on one side of each slide of bread.
    2. Place Ham in top of the the buttered side of bread1.
    3. Place bread2 with the buttered side facing the ham in top of the ham.
    4. Eat.
  3. Else, grab some water.
